    Origin

In Middle English the adverb developed the senses \xe2\x80\x98strongly, vigorously\xe2\x80\x99 (compare with run hard), and \xe2\x80\x98close, immediate\xe2\x80\x99 (just surviving in the archaic fast by; compare with hard by), hence \xe2\x80\x98closely, immediately\xe2\x80\x99 and \xe2\x80\x98quickly\xe2\x80\x99; the idea of rapid movement was then reflected in adjectival use.
