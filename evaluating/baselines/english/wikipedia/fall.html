Common generic uses[edit]

  • Fall (surname), a surname
  • Fall, academic term, usually a semester or a quarter, occurring around the same time as the autumn season
  • Fall of man, or "The Fall", the Christian doctrine of mankind's rebellion against God, as found predominantly in the Adam and Eve narrative
  • Waterfall, or falls, a geological formation, more typically referred to as "falls" than simply "fall"
  • Fall (accident), including slipping or tripping
