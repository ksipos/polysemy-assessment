Suicide is the act of intentionally ending one's own life (and can be a noun for a person who has committed suicide).

Mass suicide is a form of suicide, occurring when a group of people simultaneously kill themselves.

A victim is suicided when a murder is staged or ruled as a suicide, particularly in spycraft and statecraft.
