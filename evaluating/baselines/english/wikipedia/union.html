  • Union (Portuguese: União), the federal government of Brazil

  • Personal union, a combination of two or more states which have the same head of state (typically a monarch) whilst their boundaries, laws, and interests remain distinct
  • Real union, a union of two or more states, which share some state institutions as in contrast to personal unions but which are not as unified as entities in a political union

  • The Union, a term used to refer to the European Union
  • The Union (Russian Cyrillic: "Союз", transliterated Russian: "Soyuz"), a common internal abbreviation of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
