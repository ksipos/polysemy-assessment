    Definition of ainalite in English:

    ainalite

    noun

    rareMineralogy

    • A variety of cassiterite containing iron and tantalum.

    Origin

    Mid 19th century; earliest use found in James Dana (1813\xe2\x80\x931895), geologist, zoologist, and teacher. From Swedish ainalit, apparently irregularly from Finnish aina always, constantly + Swedish -lit.

    Pronunciation

    ainalite
