Science[edit]

  • Drive reduction theory (learning theory), a type of motivational theory
  • Drive theory, a diverse set of motivational theories in psychology
    • Motivation, the internal condition that activates behavior and gives it direction
  • Gene drive, in genetics, biased inheritance of a gene
  • Prey drive, the instinctive behavior of a carnivore to pursue and capture prey

Technology[edit]

  • Drive, a data storage device, including:

    • Optical disc drive, a disc drive that uses laser light or electromagnetic waves within or near the visible light spectrum
    • Solid-state drive, a solid-state storage device that uses integrated circuit assemblies
    • USB flash drive, a data storage device that includes flash memory with an integrated USB interface
  • Drive, a spacecraft propulsion device
  • Drive, an automobile transmission device
