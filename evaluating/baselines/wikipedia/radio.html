Radio broadcasting culture[edit]

  • Radio broadcasting, a service to transmit audio content via radio waves
  • Radio drama, audio storytelling broadcast on the radio
  • Radio format, the theme or genre of programming from a radio broadcaster
  • Radio program, the content of radio broadcasting
  • Radio programming, organizing radio programs in a daily, weekly, or season-long schedule

Science and technology[edit]

  • Radio astronomy, the study of celestial objects at radio frequencies
  • Radio equipment
  • Radio receiver, for receiving radio signals
  • Radio transmitter, for sending radio signals
  • Radio waves, the medium of radio communication
  • Shortwave radio, using high frequency radio waves
  • Two-way radio, for both sending and receiving radio signals, including:
    • Amateur radio, also called "ham radio"
    • Citizen's band radio, often shortened to "CB radio"
    • Walkie-talkie, a transceiver designed to be portable
