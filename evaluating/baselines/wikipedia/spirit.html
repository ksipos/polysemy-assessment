Spirit is the non-corporeal essence of a being or entity.

Spirit as a common noun may also refer to:

  • Mood (psychology), primarily good mood or optimism ("high spirits")
    • Enthusiasm
  • the mind or soul or Volition
    • German Geist
    • Spirituality, pertaining to the soul or spirit
  • volatile liquids, primarily ethanol
    • Liquor, or spirits, a beverage produced by distilling fermented grain, fruit, or vegetables
    • Tincture or spirit, an alcoholic solution of a volatile drug
    • Perhaps by extension, other volatile (especially flammable) liquids, such as white spirit
